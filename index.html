Frequently Asked Questions

How does net metering work in Pakistan?
We handle all LESCO/MEPCO documentation, install bi-directional meters, and ensure you get credits for excess solar energy exported to the grid.
What is the payback period for solar?
Typically 3-5 years based on current electricity rates, with system lifespan exceeding 25 years.
Do you provide maintenance services?
Yes, AMC plans available for panel cleaning, inverter checks, and full electrical safety audits.
